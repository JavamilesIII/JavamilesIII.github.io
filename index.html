About me

IT

My interest in IT began in primary school. As time passed, I kept learning more and more, and at the age of 13, I started coding with Arduino. From that point on, I coded more frequently, leading up to my entrance into this computer science middle school. After joining, I intensified my efforts—creating my first portfolio, exploring C++, and getting familiar with PHP.

But these aren't my favorites. I enjoy Java the most, and I wasn’t bad at Python either. Now, I’m developing my own app with Java and have joined the EuroParksEU project as a developer, helping to recreate the Europa-Park theme park in Minecraft.

General

My name is Loïc Matthey, and I'm 17 years old. My home is in Switzerland and I've always lived there. I’m currently a student at the computer science middle school in Basel, where I’m deepening my knowledge in computer science.

Scouts
